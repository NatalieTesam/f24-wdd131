Purpose

Are you an eight-legged arachnid in search of a safe warm home suitable for your millions of children? Well, look no farther than Arachne Real Estate, the only suitable website for Spiders searching for that dream home. In it are a range of listings based on three different factors. Landlord hospitality, nearby restaurant quality, and Expandability. Also, we have a hit critic, Natasha Romanoff, the black widow herself, rate all the spaces available. We only cater to varieties non-lethal to humans. The real purpose is to entertain people with the site while also being informational about different types of spiders.

Audience

Spiders in search of a home for a reasonable price and location. But in reality, people who are curious about what spider real estate is and who want to be entertained by the notion of spiders having a real estate agency. People of all ages who want a good laugh or who want to know a bit about different types of spiders.

Listings

Listings are available based on three criteria. Landlord Hospitality, Restaurant quality, and Expandability.

Landlord Hospitality is pretty straight forward. If the person who lives in the area with you is nice and will not bother you, or if they may decide to one day kill you on accident if you step too far from home.

Restaurant quality includes the variety and frequency of bugs that may wander into your clutches. Based on the location, there could be good air traffic or bad air traffic increasing the variety of flying insects to munch on versus if there are alot of crawling insects that may wander into your home.

And the last criteria, expandability. Expandability is just the available room to expand before it goes too far and the landlord might evict you, especially if you are a hoarder and get too greedy, or your home may expand too far and be liable to damages from nearby regular movement.
